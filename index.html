About Me

I Need a Job

Hi there! I need a job. If you've come here from my resume via a job application, thank you! Please browse my curated portfolio below and click as many links as you can!

I'm interested in entry level software engineering opportunities. I'm also open to the prospect of interning. All I need is for one company to give me a chance - will it be yours?

My foremost passions are education, robotics and games. I like building websites, especially backends. I'm a huge fan of making and using free APIs - if code can be open source, why should information APIs hide behind paywalls?

I'm looking for a company with a healthy, employee-empowering, supportive and collaborative culture, with a fantastic and diverse community of many interests. I want to work on a focused, inspiring product with a clear vision, purpose, and noble use cases. I'd like to learn and grow rapidly as a person and a professional, in an environment where I can take risks, make mistakes, and overcome challenges. Most of all, I want to have fun and be happy. Does this sound like your company? Then I want to work for your company!

My resume doesn't tell my entire story or fully capture what I'm capable of, it's just the tip of the iceberg. Schedule a Zoom call with me and you'll find out what I'm made of, who I really am and why I'll be an incredible member on any team. Why not download my most recent resume for my contact info, then drop me a line on LinkedIn or email and let's start a conversation about how I can inject my hard work and passion into your team and product.
