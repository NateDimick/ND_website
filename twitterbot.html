Tweets from my First Bot

This is my first twitterbot, @FourthInCommand. It has a lot of backstory and not a lot to show for it. I lauched the bot in January of 2020 to mock the slew of presidential candidates. The bot operates on the simple premise of graabbing two candidate's tweets and merging them on a shared word. The resulting tweet tries to balance the two source tweets, and removes all mentions, links and other media. Sometimes words get replaced with trending topics, and sometimes emoji get inserted (sidenote: the emoji generation might be the aspect of this project I'm most proud of).
