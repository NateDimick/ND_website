Campaign Ended 4/22/2020

From January to April of 2020 @FourthInCommand tweeted as if it were a candidate for president. The basic algorithm is sentance merger that pivots from sentance A to B around a shared keyword, usually "the", "of" or another common word. This usually produced incoherent garbage, which surprisingly garnered a handful of likes and few retweets.
